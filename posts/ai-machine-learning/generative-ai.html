5. Risks, Ethics & Responsible AI

Deepfakes, misinformation, and watermarks

Synthetic media detection is an arms race. C2PA content credentials and SynthID (Google DeepMind) embed invisible watermarks.

Bias, alignment and jailbreaking

LLMs reflect training data biases. Alignment via RLHF or DPO (Direct Preference Optimization) tries to mitigate harmful outputs. Adversarial prompts (‚Äújailbreaks‚Äù) remain a challenge.

Copyright and legal grey zones

Lawsuits from Getty Images, NYT, and authors question training on copyrighted data. Tools like ‚ÄúDo Not Train‚Äù registries are emerging.

Open‚Äësource vs. closed‚Äësource safety

Proponents argue open models allow external auditing; opponents fear unfettered misuse. The debate continues.

6. Hands‚ÄëOn: Your First Generative AI Pipeline

Objective: Build a RAG pipeline that answers questions based on a PDF manual, using Llama 3 (via Groq API) and ChromaDB.

# 1. extract text, 2. chunk, 3. embed, 4. retrieve, 5. generate
import chromadb
from llama_index import SimpleDirectoryReader, VectorStoreIndex
from llama_index.llms.groq import Groq

documents = SimpleDirectoryReader("manuals").load_data()
index = VectorStoreIndex.from_documents(documents)
query_engine = index.as_query_engine(llm=Groq(model="llama3-70b-8192"))
response = query_engine.query("What is the safety voltage?")
print(response)
