üìä 3. Ratings & reviews ‚Äì reputation algorithm

Both stores consider rating count and recent rating velocity. Prompt users contextually (after a positive session). iOS 16+ SKStoreReviewController API respects rate limits; Google Play‚Äôs in-app review prompt is non-intrusive. Respond to reviews ‚Äì it signals engagement and helps rankings.

    }

üìã Complete ASO checklist (2024)

  1. App title (iOS: 30 char, GP: 50 char) ‚Äì 1-2 keywords + brand.

  7. Preview video ‚Äì autoplay muted, show UI within 3 seconds.
  8. In-app events / Live Ops ‚Äì plan quarterly.
  9. Ratings & reviews ‚Äì implement native prompts, respond weekly.
  10. Localization ‚Äì at least 5 languages for high-volume apps.
